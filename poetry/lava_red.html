LAVA RED IS FUCKING DEAD (an elegy)

lava red !

i saw myself a red giant
could melt the venom
off any cosmic crisp

creation becomes charming
as charred carbon
snakeskin beckoning
sizzling boas in mud

sludge...
a fragile felicitous growth
a sanguine nub below
left weeping
deforest devalue devour
fine smooth hairs on slender fingers
sturdy oaks for slither-play
my call to action
call me QUEEN

my axe hits seven targets

simultaneous beheading

sicken me
